Privacy Policy

WebTeX runs entirely on your device, and is developed by Verimathic Labs (verimathiclabs.com). The extension scans web pages locally to detect LaTeX-style math and renders it using KaTeX. No browsing data, personal information, or rendered content leaves your device. The app does not collect analytics, does not track users, and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Permissions requested (“All Websites”) are solely required so WebTeX can render LaTeX wherever you browse. Network access is limited to downloading KaTeX font resources that are bundled with the extension.

If you have any privacy questions please contact dev@verimathiclabs.com.
