About WebTeX

WebTeX is a Safari web extension that automatically renders LaTeX math on every webpage using KaTeX. It is developed by Verimathic Labs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does WebTeX need access to “All Websites”?
Rendering LaTeX requires scanning page content. WebTeX processes everything locally and never transmits data off-device.
Where is my data stored?
Nowhere. WebTeX does not collect or store personal data.
How do I disable WebTeX?
Open Safari Extensions settings (macOS) or Settings › Safari › Extensions (iOS) and toggle WebTeX.
